Bike Maintenance Tips

Road Bikes

Regularly clean and lubricate the chain, keep tires properly inflated, and inspect brake pads for wear. Tighten all bolts before long rides.

Mountain Bikes

Check suspension regularly, clean after muddy rides, lubricate drivetrain, and examine tire tread for off-road grip.

Hybrid Bikes

Keep tires inflated, clean chain weekly, and inspect brakes and gears monthly for smooth commuting performance.

Gravel / Adventure Bikes

Wash after dusty or wet rides, check for frame cracks, and maintain both mechanical and hydraulic components regularly.

Electric Bikes

Charge battery regularly, store in a cool dry place, clean motor and sensor areas, and inspect wiring for wear or moisture damage.

Folding Bikes

Lubricate folding joints, check locking mechanisms for wear, keep chain clean, and tighten bolts frequently due to movement.

Cruiser Bikes

Keep tires pumped, apply oil to chain and coaster brake, and inspect handlebars and seatpost for rust or loosening.

BMX Bikes

Tighten headset and axle bolts, inspect pegs and chain after tricks, and clean bearings after riding in dirt or water.

Track / Fixie Bikes

Check chain tension regularly, oil the chain, inspect single gear teeth for wear, and ensure fixed gear locknuts are tight.

Recumbent Bikes

Check seat and backrest bolts, clean long chain with degreaser, and keep tires well inflated for ergonomic riding comfort.

Cargo Bikes

Inspect frame for cracks due to load, lubricate drivetrain, monitor brakes closely under heavy loads, and service wheels regularly.

Touring Bikes

Carry chain lube and patch kit on tours, check racks and pannier mounts, and adjust gear indexing regularly during long trips.

Tandem Bikes

Ensure synchronization chain is tight, check both sets of brakes, inspect frame regularly for extra stress and align wheels carefully.

Fat Bikes

Keep tire pressure low but consistent, clean sand and mud from hubs and rims, and grease headset and bottom bracket often.

Utility Bikes

Maintain lights and fenders, check basket/rack mounts, keep chain lubricated, and watch for rust on parked bikes.
